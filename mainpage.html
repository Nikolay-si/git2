I started reading “Sublime” randomly and without many expectations, although the synopsis pleased me. I even confess that I found the amount of text a bit strange, as I had only seen the cover online and imagined it to be more of an illustration book. But I must say that I was surprised from the first few pages by a really good text, I loved the language. And how good it is to be surprised like that. In those moments when you are at work, you pick up the book that is on the table, wave to the colleague next to you and say: -Man, this book is good. Well, I liked it that much. And I hope to check out your other books.

Camila Barbosa
